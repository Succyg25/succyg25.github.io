FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope. A landing page can take 1–2 weeks, while a full brand identity or complex web app can take 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline during our discovery call.

What does a project cost?

Every project is unique. We offer custom quotes based on your specific requirements, timeline, and deliverables. Reach out via the form and we'll discuss over a free consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We work with clients across Africa, Europe, and North America. Remote collaboration is seamless — we use video calls, shared project boards, and async communication effectively.

Will I own the final files and designs?

Yes. Upon full payment, you receive complete ownership of all design assets, source files, and any code we produce for your project.
